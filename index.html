Why Choose DOFF 3 Over Standard Jet Washing?

The DOFF 3 machine is an advanced cleaning system that provides superior results compared to standard jet washing. Here’s why:

  • Uses high-temperature steam (up to 150°C) rather than high pressure, making it gentle on surfaces.
  • Prevents damage to stone, brick, and delicate historical surfaces.
  • Removes algae, moss, and biological matter without using harsh chemicals.
  • Eco-friendly, as it requires minimal water and avoids excessive runoff.
  • Highly effective for graffiti removal without causing surface erosion.
