About Us

Welcome to REALM CARPENTRY, where craftsmanship and tradition come together. For over 20 years, our family-owned business has been dedicated to providing exceptional carpentry services accross LONDON, transforming homes and businesses with our expert woodworking skills.

Our journey began with a passion for woodworking passed down through generations. Today, we continue to uphold the values of quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction that have been the cornerstone of our business since its inception.

As a family-owned company, we take great pride in our work and treat every project as if it were our own. From custom furniture and cabinetry to intricate woodwork and renovations, our experienced team is committed to bringing your vision to life with precision and care.

Thank you for considering REALM CARPENTRY. We look forward to the opportunity to create something beautiful for you.

Our Services

Wardrobe Making

Whether you`re looking to maximize a small space with a new fitted wardrobe or embarking on a multi-room renovation project, we`re here to assist you every step of the way.

  • Expert Craftsmanship: Our experienced craftsmen meticulously build each wardrobe, paying attention to every detail to ensure exceptional quality.
  • Professional Installation: We provide seamless installation services, ensuring your custom wardrobe is perfectly fitted and ready for use.
  • Space Optimization: Whether you need a fitted wardrobe for a small space or a comprehensive storage solution for a larger area, we design to maximize your storage capabilities.

General Carpentry

  • Custom Woodwork: From bespoke furniture to intricate wood detailing, we create custom pieces that enhance the beauty and functionality of your space.
  • Interior and Exterior Carpentry: Whether it's installing trim, molding, or exterior features like decks and pergolas, we ensure every project is completed to the highest standards.
  • Repairs and Maintenance: We provide expert repair and maintenance services to keep your woodwork in perfect condition, extending its lifespan and preserving its beauty.
  • Renovations and Remodeling: Our team can help transform your home or business with tailored carpentry solutions that fit your style and requirements.

Roof Building

  • New Roof Installation: We offer expert installation services for new construction projects, using high-quality materials to ensure durability and longevity.
  • Roof Replacement: When it's time for a new roof, we provide seamless roof replacement services, minimizing disruption and enhancing the value of your property.
  • Custom Roofing Solutions: Whether you're looking for a specific roofing style or material, we can create a custom roofing solution that meets your aesthetic and functional needs.

Custom Furniture & Wooden Business Cards

We offer personalized design solutions tailored to your unique style and space requirements. We also specialize in creating custom shelves, doors, ensuring that every element of your space is both functional and aesthetically pleasing. Elevate your professional image with our custom-crafted wooden business cards, designed to leave a lasting impression with their unique elegance and exceptional quality.

Kitchen Remodeling

Our kitchen replacement service offers a comprehensive solution to completely revamp your kitchen space with high-quality materials and expert craftsmanship. From removing old fixtures to installing new cabinetry, countertops, flooring, and appliances, we handle every detail to create a beautiful, functional kitchen that suits your style and needs.
